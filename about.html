MAVUNOVET PRODUCTS & SERVICES

Leading Online shopping Agrovet in Eldoret

MavunoVet Agrovet Limited is a Kenyan registered enterprise with over 8 years of experience in the distribution of high-quality farm inputs, Veterinary and Agronomy consultancy services within Eldoret and major parts of Uasin Gishu County.

Agrochemicals

We have a wide range of agrochemicals, veterinary products, public health solutions, sprayers biologicals & industrial solutions for all your needs.

Farm tools & equipments

Livestock equipment, Fencing, watering systems, feeding equipment, handling tools, housing, and milking equipment are essential.

Veterinary products

MavunoVet has grown into a leading distributor of veterinary pharmaceuticals and animal health products.

Public health & fumigation equipments

Find professional-grade hand-pump sprayers, power sprayers, backpack sprayers, dusters, animal traps, safety equipment & bee suits.

Seeds

We offer a wide variety of Agricultural seeds of unbeatable quality. All our seeds have been tested and approved by KEPHIS under the Kenyan Laws.

Fertilizers

MavunoVet provides access to the largest variety of agri-inputs in Kenya ranging from agriculture chemicals, fertilisers etc.
